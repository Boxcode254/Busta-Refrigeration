ABOUT BUSTA REFRIGERATION

YOUR TRUSTED PARTNER IN COOLING SOLUTIONS

Founded in 2009, Busta Refrigeration has grown into one of Kenya's most trusted names in refrigeration, air conditioning, and cooling technology. With over 15 years of hands-on experience, we serve agricultural, hospitality, manufacturing, healthcare, and education sectors across East Africa — delivering engineered solutions tailored to every client's unique needs.
