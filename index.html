   Our Services 

COLD ROOM SOLUTIONS

Cold room allows pinpoint regulation of temperature and humidity and maintenance of constant conditions by use of a refrigeration and ventilation system. We have a wide portfolio of cold room solutions to suit the diverse capacities, temperatures, and processes – for plug-in or remote installations, in small and larger sizes. We are constantly looking to provide a better more efficient product by combining innovation with tried-and-true industry standards. From the design phase of a project to its final turn-over package, our team of experts will listen to and coordinate with you to insure a successful conclusion.

MILK CHILLERS SOLUTIONS

Milk chillers are an integral part in the dairy industry, as without them optimum pasteurization would not be possible. Cooling in the dairy industry requires coolant to draw heat out of the milk, dropping the temperature to preferred levels. Our milk cooling solutions are complete temperature control systems. From portable milk chillers to easy to install cooling jackets, we make process cooling in the dairy industry easy.

 MORGUE REFRIGERATION SOLUTIONS

Morgue refrigeration is used for the storage of human corpses at low temperatures to avoid decomposition. Our morgue refrigeration solutions vary in capacity ranging from a single body to fifteen bodies. Our ability to offer different types of morgue refrigeration solutions as per our clients needs has seen us grow and become a dominant force in the industry

AIR  CONDITIONING SOLUTIONS
